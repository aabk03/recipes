Carne con Verduras

Ingredients

  • chuck roast
  • 1 head of garlic
  • 2 yellow or white onions
  • sea salt
  • 2 tomatos
  • 3 green chiles
  • 2 potatoes

Instructions

  1. Cut chuck roast in small chunks.
  2. Place in a pot to boil with water about 3 inches above the meat.
  3. Once the water boils, take out the foam.
  4. Add a head of garlic and a whole white or yellow onion into the water.
  5. Lower heat to low boil. Leave for 3-3.5 hours (the longer, the softer the meat).
  6. About 30 min before the meat is done, add sea-salt.
  7. Once done, take out and shred meat.
  8. Dice separate onion, tomatos, green chile, and potatoes.
  9. Heat up a pan with oil and put in veggies.
  10. Once veggies are done, add shredded meat (freeze any that isn't going to be used). Add a little water if meat seems dry.
  11. Add spices: pepper, onion powder, garlic powder, tomato bouillon (if there is no leftover water from boil, add a little garlic salt).
