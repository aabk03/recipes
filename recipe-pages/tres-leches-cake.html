Tres Leches Cake

Ingredients

  • 1 box cake-mix (preferably yellow)
  • 1 small box of vanilla pudding mix
  • 16oz half and half
  • 14oz can of lechera
  • 8oz cream cheese
  • 12oz can of carnation milk
  • 8oz container of cool whip

Instructions

  1. Make box of cake-mix and add box of vanilla pudding. Bake according to instructions.
  2. For filling: blend half and half, 3/4 can of lechera, cream cheese, 1/2 can of carnation milk.
  3. While still hot - inject filling into cake until well-soaked.
  4. Once cake is cool and moist from filling, top with cool whip.
  5. Add any toppings to preference (strawberries, pecans, kiwi).
