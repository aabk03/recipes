Banana Bread

Ingredients

  • 3 very ripe bananas
  • 1 tspn baking soda
  • 1 pinch of salt
  • 1 cup of sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tspn vanilla
  • 1 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/3 cup melted butter
  • Walnuts to your liking

Instructions

  1. Preheat oven 350 degrees F.
  2. Mash bananas until smooth.
  3. Add melted butter, baking soda, salt, sugar, egg, vanilla, and flour. Mix.
  4. Pour mix into buttered pan.
  5. Bake 1 hr.
